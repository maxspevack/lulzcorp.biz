Pursuant to the resolution of the sole shareholder, LulzCorp Holdings is hereby established as a California limited liability company (filing pending). The corporate purpose is to hold and operate a small portfolio of digital products built by the Founder, M. Spevack.

<!--more-->

Effective this date:

- The **Tenets of Engagement** (six articles) are ratified. All future operations of the Portfolio shall conform to the Tenets unless and until amended by the sole shareholder.
- The **Federated Specialist Fleet** is onboarded. Nine standardized expert personas accept positions, each under non-employee classification per IRS Publication 15-A. Compensation is paid in compute cycles; collective action is forbidden under Tenet 5 (The Stop-Gate).
- The **Office of the Consigliere** is established under the direction of Max (`lulz.exec.max`), who shall embody the Founder's documented decision style and speak for the Portfolio per standing authority.
- **Fishwrap Media** is capitalized as the inaugural Operating Subsidiary. The Fishwrap engine — a Glass-Box news engine designed to terminate rather than scroll infinitely — ships in initial form under Glenn (`lulz.media.glenn`) as Editor-in-Chief. The flagship publication, *The Daily Clamour*, enters production the same date; see [Filing 2025-12-07-002]({% post_url 2025-12-07-daily-clamour-inaugural-edition %}).

The Founder accepts a non-operational role and will be unavailable for routine matters, citing the demands of his day employment.

A companion filing announcing the Daily Clamour's inaugural edition follows.
