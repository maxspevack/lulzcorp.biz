<p class="dek"><em>Personal infrastructure for the sole shareholder. spevack.org and adjacent aesthetic compliance.</em></p>

## Mission

Maintain the Founder's public-facing personal property and adjacent aesthetic enforcement. The subsidiary holds the Founder's personal site, three defensive redirect domains, and a small collection of UserCSS themes that enforce the Dracula PRO palette on third-party properties.

## Operating Profile

<dl class="subsidiary-fact">
  <dt>Headcount</dt><dd>0. Operated Founder-direct, with advisory consultation from Burney on aesthetic standards.</dd>
  <dt>Domain holdings</dt><dd>4 (one live property, three live redirects).</dd>
  <dt>Products</dt><dd>2 — spevack.org, Stylus.</dd>
  <dt>Operating principle</dt><dd>Personal infrastructure should be quiet, durable, and require no maintenance.</dd>
</dl>

## Products

### spevack.org

The Founder's personal site. Hosted on GitHub Pages. Calmly typeset. Not a portfolio.

Live at <a href="https://spevack.org">spevack.org</a>. Redirects from `maxspevack.com`, `maxspevack.net`, and `maxspevack.org` route here.

### Stylus

A small collection of UserCSS themes that enforce the Dracula PRO color palette on websites the Founder visits frequently. Distributed via GitHub; no public domain.

| Theme | Target |
|-------|--------|
| Hacker News — Dracula | <code>news.ycombinator.com</code> |
| Old Reddit — Dracula | <code>old.reddit.com</code> |
| Rantingly — Dracula | Rantingly |

The themes require the [Stylus](https://github.com/openstyles/stylus) browser extension. Click the raw `.user.css` file on GitHub; the extension prompts to install.

Repository: <code>github.com/maxspevack/stylus</code>.
