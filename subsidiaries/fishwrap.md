## The Engine

Fishwrap is a Glass-Box news engine that transforms RSS feeds, Reddit threads, and Hacker News into finite, auditable HTML editions. The engine ships as a signed OCI image. Downstream products — newsletters, intranets, personal news pages — pull the image, mount a config, and run it.

A hosted-instance offering is anticipated on `fishwrap.io`. Pricing model and timeline are undetermined. The Editor reports moderate progress.
